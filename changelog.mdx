<Update label="2026-02-28">
TikTok Commercial Music Library - browse trending tracks and attach music to video and photo posts via `musicSoundInfo`. [Docs](/api-reference/platforms/tiktok-music)

Google Business Reviews - import reviews and reply to them via API. Async import flow with monthly limits per tier. [Docs](/api-reference/platforms/google-business-reviews)

Remote disconnection detection - we check all connected accounts every 6 hours and send `social-account.updated` webhooks when tokens die. Configurable grace period via `deleteAccountAfter`. [Docs](/api-reference/webhooks#remote-disconnection-detection)

Unset channel - clear the selected page/channel without disconnecting OAuth. Switch Facebook Pages, LinkedIn Company Pages, YouTube channels, etc. without re-auth. [Docs](/api-reference/connect-social-accounts#unset-channel-switch-pages)

Twitter reply settings - control who can reply to your tweets (`EVERYONE`, `FOLLOWING`, `MENTIONED_USERS`, `SUBSCRIBERS`, `VERIFIED`). [Docs](/api-reference/platforms/twitter#reply-settings)

YouTube raw analytics - age/gender demographics and country-level view data for videos. [Docs](/api-reference/platforms/youtube#raw-analytics---demographics--audience-data)

Import filtering - filter imports by `surface` (grid, non-grid, stories) and `mediaType` (video, image). Carousel/gallery media now captured in `extraData.media[]` with `importCarousels` flag. [Docs](/api-reference/import-posts)

Analytics for imported posts - ongoing analytics refresh for imported posts (not just initial snapshot). Uses `importedPostId` in analytics endpoints.

Data retention cleanup - analytics now hard-deleted after 30 days. Imported posts cleaned after 30 days. Webhook logs after 7 days. [Docs](/api-reference/data-retention)
</Update>
