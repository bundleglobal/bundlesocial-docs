Every platform has rules. Some are sensible, some are arbitrary. We enforce them so you don't get banned.

## Validation 101

*   **Bitrate:** `(fileSize * 8) / videoLength`.

## Instagram

### Reels (The Golden Child)
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (0.56) is mandatory. Don't upload landscape.
*   **Duration:** 3s - 15min.
*   **Size:** Max 1GB.
*   **Format:** MP4 only.

### Stories
*   **Duration:** Max 60s.
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 recommended.

### Posts
*   **Images:** 4:5 (Portrait) or 1:1 (Square). Max 8MB.
*   **Videos:** Max 10 mins.

<Warning>
**Gotcha:** Instagram is SUPER strict on aspect ratios. If your pixel math is off by even a tiny bit, they might reject it.
</Warning>

## TikTok

### Videos
*   **Duration:** Max 10 mins (600s).
*   **Size:** Max 4GB.
*   **Resolution:** Min 360p.
*   **Privacy:** If you set it to `SELF_ONLY`, you won't get a share link.

### Images (Photo Mode)
*   **Count:** 1-35 images.
*   **Format:** JPG and JPEG only. TikTok does not accept PNG or other image formats.
*   **Music:** You can't pick specific tracks via API easily. We have an `autoAddMusic` flag if you feel lucky.

## YouTube

### Shorts
*   **Duration:** Max 60s.
*   **Ratio:** Square (1:1) or Vertical (9:16).
*   **Logic:** If it fits these rules, YouTube *automatically* makes it a Short.

### Long Form
*   **Size:** Max 256GB (Yes, really).
*   **Duration:** Max 12 hours.

---

## LinkedIn

### Video
*   **Size:** Max 200MB (via API).
*   **Duration:** 3s - 30min.

### Document (PDF)
*   **Size:** Max 100MB.
*   **Pages:** Max 300.
*   **Format:** PDF only.

---

## Twitter / X

### Video
*   **Size:** 512MB.
*   **Duration:** 
    *   Free/Basic: 140s.
    *   Premium: 10 mins.
    *   Premium+: 2 hours (supposedly, but API varies).

### Images
*   **Count:** Max 4 per tweet.
*   **Size:** Max 5MB.

---

## Facebook

### Reels
*   **Duration:** 3s - 60s.
*   **Ratio:** 9:16.

### Page Posts
*   **Images:** Max 4MB.
*   **Videos:** Max 4GB.

---

## Pinterest

### Pins
*   **Images:** Max 20MB.
*   **Videos:** Max 2GB. 15 mins max.
*   **Board ID:** Required. You can't pin to thin air.

---

## The "Other Guys"

### Reddit
*   **Video:** Max 1GB. 15 mins max.
*   **Galleries:** Allowed on some subreddits.
*   **Flair:** Required if the subreddit demands it.

### Threads
*   **Video:** Max 5 mins.
*   **Images:** Max 10 items.
*   **Text:** 500 chars.

### Mastodon
*   **Video:** Max 99MB.
*   **Text:** Instance dependent (usually 500 chars).
