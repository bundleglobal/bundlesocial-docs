Keeping a steady content rhythm is hard. A simple calendar makes it easy. This guide gives you a free 2026 content calendar template and a clear process to plan posts that grow reach and save time.

<h2>How to use it in 15 minutes each week</h2>
<h3>1. Pick a monthly theme</h3>
Choose a simple theme that supports your goals. Awareness, launch, education, community, or support.

<h3>2. Draft 6 to 12 post ideas</h3>
Mix formats. One educational post. One story. One proof. One community prompt. One short video. One carousel or thread.

<h3>3. Match ideas to platforms</h3>
Repurpose smartly. Turn a how to into a Reel, a TikTok, and a LinkedIn post. Keep the hook native to each platform.

<h3>4. Schedule and automate</h3>
Batch schedule for the week so you are not stuck posting at odd hours.
