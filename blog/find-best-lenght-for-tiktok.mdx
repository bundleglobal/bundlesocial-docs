## What is the Ideal TikTok Video Length?
With over 1.534 billion users worldwide, TikTok has become one of the most popular
platforms, especially among users aged 19 to 29. While TikTok has expanded its maximum
video length from 60 seconds to 10 minutes, research still shows that shorter videos—typically
between 15 and 60 seconds—are the sweet spot for maximizing engagement.

According to WIRED, videos lasting between 21 and 34 seconds see the highest levels of engagement. While longer videos have their place, TikTok’s fast-paced nature and user preferences make short, snappy content the most effective way to capture attention.
