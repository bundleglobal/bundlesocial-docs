<h2>Help your videos rank in TikTok search</h2>
TikTok is a search engine for a huge part of your audience. People type recipes, tutorials, reviews, and local queries. You can earn consistent views by making your videos easy to find.

<h2>The checklist</h2>
<h3>1. Find real keywords</h3>
- Type your topic into TikTok search and note the autosuggest phrases
- Check “Others searched for” on result pages
- Save 5 to 10 phrases that match your video

<h3>2. Write a clear title hook</h3>
- Say the main keyword out loud in the first 3 seconds
- Put the exact phrase in your on screen text
- Keep it short, strong, and readable on mobile

<h3>3. Add a matching caption</h3>
- Use your main phrase near the start
- Add 2 to 3 related phrases in simple sentences
- Keep it natural and helpful

<h3>4. Use focused hashtags</h3>
- 3 to 5 relevant tags are enough
- Mix one broad tag with niche tags
- Skip generic tags that add noise

<h3>5. Nail retention and replays</h3>
- Cut silence at the start
- Add pattern breaks every 2 to 4 seconds
- Use jump cuts and captions to keep pace
- A tight 15 to 30 second video often wins

<h3>6. Choose audio wisely</h3>
- Use sounds that fit your topic
- Trend audio can help if it matches the content

<h3>7. Set the right cover</h3>
- Add a readable title on the cover frame
- Make it match the keyword and promise

<h3>8. Encourage comments</h3>
- Ask a specific question at the end
- Pin a helpful comment with extra context

<h3>9. Post at active times</h3>
- Share when your audience is online
- Early engagement helps your rank

<h3>10. Track and refine</h3>
- Watch views from search in your analytics
- Update captions on underperformers to test new phrases

<h2>Template you can copy</h2>
Intro line: say the main phrase on screen and in audio.  
Middle: deliver steps or proof in clear beats.  
Close: ask a question, promise a follow up, or point to a part two.  
Caption: one line summary with the main phrase, then two support lines.  
Tags: one broad tag, three niche tags, and a location tag if relevant.
