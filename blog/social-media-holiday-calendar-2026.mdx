<h2>Your 2026 holiday roadmap for easy content wins</h2>
Holidays give you timely hooks and fresh angles. Use this month by month guide to
plan ahead. Keep posts simple, relevant, and true to your brand.

<h2>January</h2>- New Year’s Day: Goals, resets, and templates - National Clean Your
Desk Day: Workspace tips - Post idea: Share your 3 step audit for a better start

<h2>February</h2>- Valentine’s Day: Customer love and shoutouts - Super Bowl weekend:
Game day snacks or gear - Post idea: Partner spotlight with a quick story

<h2>March</h2>- International Women’s Day: Founder or team stories - Start of Spring:
Fresh launches and updates - Post idea: Three resources that helped you grow

<h2>April</h2>- Earth Day: Sustainability notes or packaging choices - Tax Day: Tips
for freelancers and small teams - Post idea: One thing you changed to reduce waste

<h2>May</h2>- Mother’s Day: Gratitude and gift guides - Graduation season: Advice
for beginners - Post idea: Starter kit list in 20 seconds

<h2>June</h2>- Pride Month: Support and inclusive resources - Mid year: Review and
roadmap - Post idea: Three lessons from the first half of the year

<h2>July</h2>- Summer: Light content, lists, and quick demos - Post idea: What we
are reading or watching this month

<h2>August</h2>- Back to school: Organizing and planning - Post idea: Five time saving
tips for busy weeks

<h2>September</h2>- Back to business: Deep dives and frameworks - Post idea: A mini
case study in 30 seconds

<h2>October</h2>- Halloween: Fun twists, costumes, themed edits - Post idea: Spooky
myths in your niche, truth vs fiction

<h2>November</h2>- Black Friday and Cyber Monday: Bundles and bonuses - Gratitude
posts: Community thanks - Post idea: Early access list with clear value

<h2>December</h2>- Year in review: Milestones and highlights - Holiday gifting: Curated
picks - Post idea: Three best performing posts and what you learned

<h2>How to plan with bundle.social</h2>- Schedule across platforms with one flow
- Track results to see which holidays move the needle

<h2>Keep it simple</h2>
Pick two holidays each month. Tie your post to a real customer moment. Use a clear
hook and a short caption. Then schedule and move on.
